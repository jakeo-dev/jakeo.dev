Wrongly Measure your Self-Worth with these Potentially Useful Grades-Related Calculations Other than GPA

July 2023

Numbers can be used to help humans understand the universe, but also more incorrectly to measure their superiority or inferiority compared to other people. Numbrs in the education world are used a lot to determine how well one is doing in a particular course, subject, year, or school overall. Grade point average (GPA) is a useful number to see how well one is performing on average each period, but it has its flaws, so I've come up with some new methods to calculate your abilities to hopefully understand how you rank among your peers.

Enter your coursework, activities, and tests to automatically complete some of these calculations by using Plannter.
